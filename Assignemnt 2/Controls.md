#Controls 

- Basic Movement:
  - Up: [W]
  - Down: [S]
  - Left: [A]
  - Right: [D]
  - Sprint Toggle: [Left_Shift]   
  - Crouch: [Left Alt]
  - Jump: [Space]
   
   
- Weapons: 
  - Toggle Weapon Menu: [Q]
  - Cycle Weapon Menu: [Mouse Wheel]
  - Aim: [Right Mouse Button]
        
- Camera
  - Mouse
  - Change Camera to 1st/3rd Person: [Hold C]
  - Switch Shoulders: [Tap C]
  - Slowmotion: [Z]
